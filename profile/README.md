### 👋 About Us

EBO Computers and Trading exists to **recalibrate optimal technology solutions** for the restaurant and hospitality industry. From POS systems to complete back-of-house automation, we help restaurants across **Pakistan, Bahrain, and Saudi Arabia** run faster, smarter, and more profitably.

- 🏢 **Founded:** 2022
- 🌍 **Headquarters:** Rawalpindi, Pakistan — active in Bahrain & Saudi Arabia
- 🎯 **Vision:** To be the leading provider of innovative technology solutions for the hospitality industry
- 🤝 **Trusted by:** KFC, McDonald's, Subway, Burger King, Domino's, and 100+ restaurant brands
